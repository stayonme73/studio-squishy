# Studio Squishy — Digital Art Prism (Founder Approved)

**Status:** Approved for Phase 1 representation  
**Phase:** Build exploration — refine only  
**Studio interior:** Locked — entrance corridor only

---

## Purpose

This is **not** permanent artwork.

The Digital Art Prism is a **dynamic digital installation** designed to celebrate creativity, showcase local artists, and keep the Studio experience feeling alive.

---

## Concept

**Three-sided vertical digital prism.**

Acts as a **rotating creative beacon** within the entrance experience.

Phase 1: static imagery placeholder communicating future intent.

---

## Placement (founder locked — June 2026)

| Rule | Direction |
| ---- | --------- |
| **Location** | Entrance corridor — **former statement planter position** (left side, past welcome wall) |
| **Removed** | Statement planter — **eliminated** to preserve coffee nook visibility and access |
| **Coffee nook** | Right side — must remain **fully visible and unobstructed** |
| **Portal** | End of hall — clear sightline maintained |
| **Circulation** | Comfortable flow around installation — no hallway congestion |

**Rationale:** The statement planter blocked sightlines and access to the coffee nook. The prism replaces that footprint with a slimmer vertical installation that preserves openness.

---

## Scale

Approximately:

- **9 feet tall** — human-scale but dramatic
- **Elegant footprint** — slim enough to preserve corridor openness
- Premium materials · clean contemporary silhouette

## Art direction

The prism should evoke:

- Curiosity
- Discovery
- Conversation
- Creative energy

**Content tone:** Urban · quirky · local artist energy · clever visual metaphors — same fun-art direction as entrance wall art.

| Yes | No |
| --- | --- |
| Street-art influence · local artists | Corporate advertisements |
| Thought-provoking — *"Wait, what does that mean?"* | Generic stock imagery |
| Playful visual metaphors | Inspirational poster aesthetics |

**Success test:** Guests stop to look, smile, discuss, or take photos.  
**Failure test:** Guests walk past without noticing.

---

## Design requirements

**Maintain:**

- Clean contemporary silhouette
- Premium materials
- Subtle lighting integration
- Visual harmony with Studio Squishy teal architectural language

**Avoid:**

- Oversized footprints
- Blocking the coffee nook
- Blocking the portal destination
- Creating hallway congestion

---

## Plants (updated)

- **No statement planter** at prism location
- Minimal supporting greenery elsewhere only if needed — no jungle

---

## Founder intent

The prism symbolizes that creativity is constantly evolving.

The Studio should never feel static. There should always be something new to discover.
