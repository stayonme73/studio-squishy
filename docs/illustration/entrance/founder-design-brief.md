# Studio Squishy — Entrance Design Brief (Founder Approved)

**Phase:** Build exploration — refine only, do not redesign  
**Studio interior:** Locked  
**Master entrance:** Hybrid — PIC 2 layout + PIC 1 portal DNA (portal at end of hall)

---

## Objective

Energetic, inspiring, innovative, welcoming — **Studio Squishy identity** carried from main studio.

**Feel:** Innovative + Creative + Contemporary + Warm + Inspiring

**Not:** Luxury hotel · law office · corporate coworking clone

> People walk in and think: **"I want to create something here."**

**Design test:** *"I can't wait to get started"* — not *"Be careful, don't spill anything."*

---

## Layout — hallway

Refine only: **wider · longer · open · comfortable traffic flow · membership growth ready**

No cramped corridors.

---

## Flooring

**Approved:** Polished concrete  
**Not:** Rugs · office carpet · warm wood plank

---

## Ceiling

**Approved:** Teal architectural lighting (Studio pipe language inspired)  
**Refine:** More dramatic · more artistic · clean contemporary lines  
**Not:** Literal exposed pipes

---

## Workspace corridor

**Approved:** Frosted glass fronts · minimal metal framing · slim hardware  
**Refine:** Less dark heavy trim · more visible frosted glass — invitation, not restriction

---

## Portal (end of hall)

**Approved:** Large portal at **end** — destination moment  
**Frame:** Brushed steel (Studio portal language) · dramatic scale  
**Scenery:** Vibrant city sunrise · mountain sunrise · coastal overlook · elevated viewpoints  
**Not:** Dense forests · generic landscapes · dark environments

---

## Welcome wall

**Refine:** Brighter · colorful · interactive feel · Studio Squishy branding  
**May show:** Welcome · weather · workspace availability · Today's Spark · announcements  
**Tone:** Creative · encouraging · optimistic — not corporate bulletin board

---

## Digital Art Prism (approved)

**Three-sided vertical digital prism** ~9 ft tall · rotating creative beacon · Phase 1 static imagery placeholder

**Placement:** Entrance corridor — **former statement planter position** (left, past welcome wall)

**Preserve:** Clear coffee nook visibility · clear portal sightline · comfortable circulation

Celebrate local artists · fresh energy · community anticipation · curiosity · discovery · conversation

See [digital-art-prism.md](./digital-art-prism.md) for full spec.

---

## Art direction (founder approved)

**Fun art means:**

| Yes | No |
| --- | --- |
| Urban · unexpected · conversation starters | Beige abstract rectangles |
| Slightly rebellious · quirky · contemporary | Hotel / attorney / corporate decor |
| Clever visual metaphors | Generic motivational posters |
| Street-art influence · local artists | Safe stock photography |
| Thought-provoking — *"Wait, what does that mean?"* | Sterile minimalism |

**Reference tone (not literal copy):** messy-creativity attitude · clever mindset metaphors · stencil/urban curiosity — human, bold, memorable.

Rotating displays and wall art should feel like **Photo 1–3 energy** — not those exact pieces in production without rights.

---

## Coffee nook

**Refine:** Playful industrial creativity  
**Approved ideas:** Cargo-container inspired base · black metal · walnut accents · fun mugs · Studio Squishy to-go cups · personality and surprise

**Mood:** *"Take a break. Then go build something amazing."*  
**Not:** Hospital break room · luxury hotel coffee station

---

## Plants

**Statement planter removed** — replaced by Digital Art Prism at same location (coffee nook visibility)

Minimal supporting greenery only elsewhere if needed (e.g. tiny touches at coffee shelf)  
**Not:** Jungle · hanging vine overload · large planters blocking key zones

---

## Palette

**Maintain:** Teal · warm neutrals · walnut · brushed steel  
**Increase:** Energy · color moments · playfulness  
**Avoid:** Dark charcoal dominance · attorney-office palette

---

## Squishy presence (reference — not build in this phase)

New visitors: warm greeting after arrival. Established members: subtler. Helpful, not overwhelming.

---

## Future member experience (reference — not systems design)

25–30 workspaces · member choice · monthly retention while current.
